# ETF Income Dashboard

Interactive comparison of 14 high-yield ETFs organized by strategy tier — from pure dividend growth to aggressive covered call funds.

## What's Inside

- **14 ETFs** compared across yield, tax efficiency, upside potential, and risk
- **4 Tiers**: Pure Dividend → Selective Covered Call → Core S&P 500 Calls → Aggressive Nasdaq/Small Cap
- Click any ETF for full breakdown: mechanism, pros/cons, tax notes, best use case
- **Quick Guide tab** with situation-based recommendations and popular pairing combinations
